Lecture 1 Introduction

Why Networks?

Many Types of Data are Networks and Graphs

Networks: A general language for describing complex systems of interating entities.（描述复杂系统中的关联实体）
Two types of Networks/Graphs:

  1. Networks (Natural Graph): Society, Communication systems, Interaction between genes/proteins
  2. Information Graphs: Information, Knowledge, Similarity networks

Networks and Applications

Questions: 网络系统是怎样组织的，如何描述网络的属性，如何利用网络的关联结构等
Ways to analyze networks:

  1. 对结点的属性进行预测（Node classification）
  2. 预测两个结点之间是否存在连接边（Link prediction）
  3. 识别紧密联系的结点簇（Community detection）
  4. 衡量结点或者网络之间的相似性（Network similarity）

Sturcture of Graphs

Definition: A network is a collection of objects where some pairs of objects are connected by links
Components:
Object: nodes (结点), vertices (顶点)，用VVV表示
Interactions: links, edges (边)，用EEE表示
System: network, graph，用G(V,E)G(V,E)G(V,E)表示

Representing Graphs

  1. Adjacency Matrix (邻接矩阵)
  2. Edge list (图中所有边的集合)
  3. Adjacency List (邻接表，适合表示大的稀疏矩阵)

    大多数Real-world Network是稀疏的 (E≪Emax,kˉ≪N−1E \ll E_{max}, \bar{k} \ll N-1E≪Emax​,kˉ≪N−1), 网络的Density为EN2\frac{E}{N^2}N2E​
    Edge Attributions: Weight (frequency of communication), Ranking (best/second best friend…), Type (friend, co-worker), Sign: Trust vs. Distrust......
    More Types of Graphs：加权图，自环图，多重图

    Connectivity of Graphs
    Connected (undirected) graph: 任意两个结点间存在路径 (path)，连通性可以通过邻接矩阵反映
    Large Component: 极大连通分支，无向图中两两结点相互连通的最大子图
    Bridge edge: 如果去掉这条边 →\rightarrow→ disconnected
    Articulation node: 如果去掉这个结点 →\rightarrow→ disconnected
    Strongly connected directed graph: 强连通图，有向图两两结点间存在双向的路径
    Weakly connected directed graph: 弱连通图，不考虑有向图中边的方向
    Strongly connected components (SCCs): 强连通分支
